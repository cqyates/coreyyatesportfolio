About Me

I am web developer with 15 years experience building website and an online presence using content management systems. My main webpage; www.vintage4me2.com is a shopify webstore that sells vintage sewing patterns. I built this business from an ebay account to a four platform webstore. In the process I discovered that I love coding and front end web development. 13 years after getting my Bachelors of Art in History from the University of California at Berkeley, I got a certificate in Full Stack Web Development from Georgia Tech.

After a 5 week intership with a local web development company, Oakworks, I am now looking for contract and remote web development projects to sink my teeth into. I also tutor students new to coding at local rec center through Codebar. I specialize in the MERN stack and do most of my current projects in react or react-native for mobile applications

I have 15 years experience in e-commerce. As the owner and operator of Vintage4me2, I have developed a web presence on Ebay and Etsy and developed my own Shopify store.

I am a small business owner so I have many jobs that contribute to building our website. I handle inventory tracking and am responsible for our growing email list and email campaigns. I write our ads with the help of programs that test keywords for SEO optimization. I both buy ads and run social media campaigns that generate traffic organically.

As a long time crafter I know what applications pattern collectors and costumers need. I have two applications in development. The first one is a MERN stack application that will create a large database of pattern catalog pages that users can search to find a catalog reference to a pattern, this service will enable costumers to date their patterns down to the season. I plan on releasing this application as a subscription service to fellow historical fashion enthusiasts in January 2020. I am also in development on another MERN stack application that will allow pattern collectors to keep a record of their patterns with images and information that will make it easy to search later. In the end this application will be integrated with my website so buyers can seamlessly add their new pattern to their collection.
