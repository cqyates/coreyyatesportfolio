About Me

My name is Corey Yates. I have 15 years experience running my own boutique webstore and I am looking to take my career to the next level. I graduated from the University of California at Berkeley in 2006 with a BA in History. I brought together my love of history, fashion and web design to create Vintage4me2. We have grown from a single eBay to account to a four platform business with our own website. I want to take the experience I am gaining through Georgia Tech's full stack web development program, my long history of experience in web design and start designing applications and websites for businesses of all sizes.
